STABLE AND STURDY, READY TO RUIN.

The Nike Metcon 7 is the most reliable and durable weight training shoe. To increase comfort, we've added responsive foam to the underfoot and a tab that locks your laces so you don't have to worry about them falling off while you focus on your next PR.

Learn more See images

Stability for Lifts

An inner plate distributes the weight evenly from edge to edge, making the wide, flat heel even more stable. It can also bend to allow for comfort during high-intensity sets.

Spring in Your Step

Nike React foam is a cushioned, lightweight base that feels springy and light when it's time to go for sprints and cardio.

Grab From All Angles

For rope climbing, revamped rubber wraps around your arch. Rubber tread provides traction from the floor of the training room to the pavement.

Non-Distracting Shoelaces

The laces are secured by a tab with a hook and loop closure. Due to the fact that these laces are made of excess material, colors may vary.

Packed With More Benefits

Lightweight mesh with textured overlays in high-wear areas helps keep your foot cool without sacrificing durability.

Tab locks down the laces with a hook-and-loop closure to help keep them out of the way.

Handstand clip at the heel minimizes drag during wall exercises.

Flywire cables integrate with the laces to help secure your midfoot. Grooves in the sole let your forefoot bend naturally.
